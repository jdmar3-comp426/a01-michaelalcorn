Origin of Oni

Oni originates from the word "on" which means to "hide or conceal". Oni are able to hide and conceal themselves from most things.
Oni during the 10th century had a different meaning than they used to. The most wicked of individuals on Earth, transform into Oni when they die.
If you are even worse, then you turn into an Oni before you die which is where folktales of Oni come from.
